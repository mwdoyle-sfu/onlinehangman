How to Play:

The computer displays a word; the player tries to guess what it is one letter at a time. The computer displays a number of dashes equivalent to the number of letters in the word.

If a guessing player suggests a letter that occurs in the word, the computer fills in the blanks with that letter in the right places.

If the word does not contain the suggested letter, the computer adds to the incorrect answer tally.

A total of 7 incorrect guesses are allowed before the game ends.
